Abstract

        This paper explores various types of bridges in civil engineering, focusing on their design, construction, and impact on connectivity and urban development. 
        Bridges play a crucial role in enhancing transportation by providing safe and efficient passage over obstacles such as rivers, valleys, and other transportation routes.
        The study reviews the characteristics, applications, and advantages of different bridge types including beam bridges, arch bridges, truss bridges, and suspension bridges, with a focus on their relevance in modern civil engineering projects.
